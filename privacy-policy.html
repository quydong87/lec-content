Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to LeC Content, an internal content automation and management tool developed for LeC Studio. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users who connect their TikTok channels to our system.

This Privacy Policy explains how LeC Content collects, uses, stores, and protects data when you use our application, specifically in connection with the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Data We Collect and Access

To provide automated video publishing features, LeC Content requests specific permissions through the TikTok OAuth 2.0 flow. We access the following information:

  • Basic Profile Information (user.info.basic scope): We access your TikTok username and profile icon to display your connected channel inside the LeC Content local management panel.
  • Video Upload Initialization (video.upload scope): We use this permission to initialize the direct-post video upload process via the TikTok Content Posting API before the video is finalized.
  • Video Publish Permission (video.publish scope): We use this permission to finalize and publish the prepared video content to your connected TikTok account after authorization.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Strict Local Storage Policy: Unlike SaaS products, LeC Content is an internal utility designed to run locally. All authorization tokens, client credentials, and metadata are stored entirely on the local storage of your host machine (specifically inside the local configuration files like tokens.json and databases like reporting.db).

We do not transmit your credentials, access tokens, or personal data to any external server. All API communication occurs directly between your local machine and the official TikTok API endpoints (https://open.tiktokapis.com).

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your access tokens, profile information, or video content with any third party. Data is only used to fulfill the automated publishing actions requested by authorized users of LeC Studio.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

You have full control over your connected accounts. You can revoke authorization for LeC Content at any time by:

  • Going to your TikTok account settings, navigating to authorized apps, and removing permissions for LeC Content.
  • Deleting the locally stored token file (tokens.json) from your installation directory, which immediately destroys all access credentials.

6. Data Retention

Since LeC Content operates entirely locally, data is retained on the host machine for as long as the software is installed and in use. Specifically:

  • OAuth Access Tokens: Stored locally and valid until revoked by the user or expired by TikTok (typically 24 hours for access tokens). Refresh tokens are stored until manually deleted.
  • Publishing Logs: Activity logs stored in the local SQLite database (reporting.db) are retained for operational auditing purposes and may be deleted at any time by the user.
  • No cloud retention: We do not retain any of your data on external or cloud servers. All data is under the direct control of the user on their local machine.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our operational procedures or regulations. Any changes will be posted directly on this page with an updated modification date.
